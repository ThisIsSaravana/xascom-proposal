Pain Points

What You Can’t Do Today

No Carrier Comparison

Can't see which carrier brings the most premium, clients, or renewals at a glance

No Growth Tracking

No way to see if premium volume is growing or shrinking month-over-month

No Product Mix View

Can't tell which products (COMP, FHO, PA) make up your revenue

No Renewal Pipeline

No view of policies expiring in next 30/60/90 days across carriers

Manual Reformatting

Each carrier sends files in different formats — reformatted manually every month

No MIS Reports

No way to generate structured reports without manually compiling from sheets
